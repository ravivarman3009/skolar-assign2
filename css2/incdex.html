PEACOCK

The peacock is the most exquisite bird in the world
because of its vibrant feathers and colour
The peacock is the national bird of India and national
pride for all of its citizens.
Peacocks mainly thrive by eating plants and small animals
and are one of the largest birds in the country.
Peacocks are ground-feeders that eat insects, plants, and
small creatures.
There are two familiar peacock species.
The blue peacock lives in India and Sri Lanka, while the green
peacock is found in Java and Myanmar (Burma).
A more distinct and little-known species, the Congo peacock,
inhabits African rain forests.
